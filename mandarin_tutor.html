Chinese Characters — Introduction

汉 字 means "Chinese Characters" — the building blocks of the language. Each character's pronunciation is a consonant + one/two vowels, or vowels only. The phonics system is Pinyin (拼音).

Note: zh/ch/sh vs j/q/x

zh = "j"(jeep)+"r"; ch = "q"(cheese)+"r"; sh = "x"(sharp)+"r". Mixing them up is not a big problem to be understood in China.
